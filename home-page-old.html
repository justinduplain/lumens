SCF Lifelong Learning and Workforce Development

In order to register for classes, you must create a Learner Profile.  Select "login/create account" in the upper left to get started.
 
To view courses, select "all classes" (or one of the categories) in the left navigation or view our .

Students will be required to bring and wear masks in all open access/common spaces where six feet of safe distancing may not be possible.
  • Students will be required to wear masks in all classrooms and instructional locations. Student non-compliance with this requirement will be treated the same as any other classroom Code of Conduct violation.
  • Any decorations on the material used to make or that are applied to the facemasks should not contain offensive language; drug, alcohol, or tobacco use; sexual images; or violent or racist images.
  • Face masks should only be removed in the classroom when manufactured Personal Protective Equipment (PPE) is required to be worn in its place for specific coursework or operational requirements.

 
If we are not currently offering a class, we can add you to our waitlist.  If you do not see the class you are looking for, please call (941) 363-7203 for assistance.  Thank you!
